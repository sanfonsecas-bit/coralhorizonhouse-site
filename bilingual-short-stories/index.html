How the book works

Spanish and English, side by side.

Each story appears in both languages on the same spread, so children can read in their stronger language and naturally pick up the other — guessing meanings from the context, recognizing new words, and building confidence one page at a time.

No drills. No flashcards. Just stories worth coming back to.

Perfect for kids ages 6–7
